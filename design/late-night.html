ben kogan: design: late night

*

late night poster
sketch.app
october 2013

this poster was created as part of my application to what is now my position on the editorial staff of 4×4 literary magazine. the entire image was created from scratch, and the "late night" text was hand-lettered.

per the application guidelines, i came up with the theme "late night" for a potential open-mic event. the "late night" text was inspired by retro neon light signage. the light globes in the background were inspired by the saint laurent spring 2014 menswear show stage.

i intended set off the slightly kitsch lettering with the sleek light matrix behind.
