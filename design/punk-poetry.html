ben kogan: design: punk poetry

*

punk poetry poster
sketch.app
december 2013

this poster was created for 4×4 magazine's punk poetry event, which combined a literary open mic with a punk music show co-hosted by WBAR.

typography was inspired by malcolm garrett's designs for punk rock group buzzcocks. the background graphic was inspired by charles peterson's grunge rock photography from his book touch me i'm sick.

i aimed to create a design in line with 4×4 magazine's modernist visual identity, but still maintain a visual connection to the history of punk.
